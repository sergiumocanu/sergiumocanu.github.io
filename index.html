About me

I'm a software engineer working at SickKids hospital in Toronto. I work with Machine Learning and Deep Learning models in Python and R. Particularly, I work on analyzing brain signals captured with MEG and converting command-line programs into user-friendly web-apps.

I have recently graduated with a Master of Applied Science (research-based) in Biomedical Engineering from Ryerson University. My research focus was on medical image analysis using a mix of computer vision and deep learning approaches. The title of my thesis was Optical Flow-Based Image Registration in FLAIR MRI.
